Adoption FAQs

Q. Where do the pets in the center come from?

A. Many are strays people bring in, others were previous pets.

Q. Does the center put a "time limit" on the animals?

A. No. All animals are here until they are adopted or fostered out.

Q.Is there an adoption application to fill out?

A. Yes. The application has a few questions concerning previous or current pets, living situation, if you know anything about the animal you are considering, etc.

Q. Where can I get an application for adoption?

A. Currently we only have paper applcations at the office. We are working toward getting online applications up and running soon.

Q. Can I bring my current pets or children to meet the animal I am considering?

A.Children and other family are always welcome to come meet the potential new family member, as are other pets as long as they are up to date on their shots and are not currently sick.

Q. Are there opportuinities to volunteer or foster the animals?

A. Yes. Volunteers are always needed and do anything from walking/playing with the pets to bathing them. Fostering is also always welcome, but you must fill out an application available at the front desk.
