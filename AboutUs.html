About Us

We are a non-profit animal shelter. We will take dogs, cats most large farm animals, many small animals and even some small exotics, we also take reptiles and birds. On premises we can house up to 20 dogs, 30 cats and many large and small animals. We also foster out animals frequently to make room for as many animals that need homes as possible.
